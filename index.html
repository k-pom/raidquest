Setup

  • Each player chooses a class and takes all of their class cards
  • Set the 3 Basic cards aside; that is your starting hand. Shuffle the rest together
  • Shuffle the monsters together, and reveal a number of minions equal to the number of players

Gameplay

Choose a random player to start. Play continues clockwise.
  • Refresh: Return all cards with 0 cooldown counters to your hand. Remove 1 counter from all of your cards in play.
  • Activate: Each player may play an ability or item from hand. If it is an ability, place a number of cooldown counters on it equal to its cost. Limit one ability per turn.
  • Attacked: Each minion in play attacks (as designated on the minion). Each minion will only attack once
  • More Enemies: Put the top card of the minion deck in play. Skip this step if there are more minions than players

Leveling up and winning

When a minion is killed, the party levels up! Each hero draws the top card of their deck and a new minion is played.

If everyone in the party is killed, the Minions win.

If the overlord (The Necromancer) is killed, the party wins.
